Background

What's your favourite subject? I can't answer that. I originally entered uWaterloo pursuing a degree in Computer Science, but made the decision to switch majors to Systems Design midway through second year. I've since strengthened my programming skills (JavaScript, HTML/CSS, Java, SQL) and have found a love and appreciation for data and analytics. The latter was gained while interning at A9.com (an Amazon subsidiary). I've attained a deep understanding of user experience needs through other experiences interning in the mobile application industry (Xtreme Labs, Zynga). Studying Systems Design has truly nurtured my appetite for taking on problems with a holistic approach.
